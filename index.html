The Legend

Where Prestige
Meets Personality

The Mac Daddy Santa Masters is a private golf pool rooted in the tradition and prestige of The Masters Tournament at Augusta National. Born from a love of the game and a refusal to take it too seriously, this is golf at its finest — with attitude.

Every April, Participants submit their picks for the field. The scores roll in. The leaderboard shifts. And when the last putt drops on 18, only one can don the metaphorical green jacket.

"The Commissioner runs the pool. The field runs the scores.
The azaleas run the vibes."

How It Works

Pay your entry fee and join the field before the deadline. The Commissioner locks picks when the first round tees off Thursday morning.

Select golfers from the official Augusta field. Your Picks are your army — choose wisely. The Field is deep, the cuts are brutal, and Mac Daddy Santa is watching.

The Authority

The
Commissioner

Every great tournament has a host club. Every great pool has a Commissioner. Ours wears the green jacket with an authority that would make Augusta's membership committee take notes.

The Commissioner sets the rules, locks the picks, enforces the deadlines, and signs off on every payout. His word is final. His jacket is earned. His scorecard is flawless.

"The Commissioner does not accept late entries.
The Commissioner does not negotiate."
