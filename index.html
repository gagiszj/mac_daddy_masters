How It Works

01
🏌️
Enter the Pool

Pay your entry fee and join the field before the deadline. The Commissioner locks picks when the first round tees off Thursday morning.

02
📋
Make Your Picks

Select golfers from the official Augusta field. Your Picks are your army — choose wisely. The Field is deep, the cuts are brutal, and Mac Daddy Santa is watching.

03
📊
Watch the Board

Track every round on the live leaderboard. Scores update in real time as the drama unfolds across Augusta's legendary 18 holes.

04
🏆
Claim the Jacket

When the final round concludes on Sunday, the Participant with the lowest combined score claims the prize pool and the eternal bragging rights.
